Smallworld Smallband

SmallWorld SmallBand (SWSB), often referred to as "ក្រុមតូច" in Khmer, is a Cambodian musical
group known for creating original music that blends modern sounds with local cultural influences.
Formed with the goal of bringing a fresh and authentic voice to the Cambodian music scene, the
band has gained popularity for their unique approach to Cambodian pop music. Their lyrics often
reflect personal stories, social issues, and experiences within the country, aiming to resonate deeply
with local audiences.


SmallWorld SmallBand is a popular Cambodian music group known for its distinct blend of indie,
pop, and rock sounds infused with Cambodian cultural elements. The band has gained significant
popularity, especially among the youth, for its relatable lyrics, unique style, and high-energy performances.

Musical Style

The band's music combines traditional Cambodian elements with Western genres like indie rock, pop, and sometimes even reggae.Their lyrics often focus on themes like love, social issues, daily life, and the struggles and dreams of young Cambodians

Popular Songs

SmallWorld SmallBand has produced several hit songs that are widely appreciated in Cambodia, such as “Knhom Min Sok Chet Te” (I’m Not Happy) and “Som Kan Ban” (Please).

Future Prospects

Given their success and popularity, SmallWorld SmallBand is expected to continue influencing Cambodia’s evolving music scene. They aim to reach an international audience and bring Cambodian indie music to the global stage.
