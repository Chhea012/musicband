ABOUT US

SmallWorld SmallBand, known in Cambodia as "ក្រុមតូច," is a popular Cambodian music group that blends traditional Cambodian musical elements with modern pop and rock. ABOUT US

SmallWorld SmallBand (SWSB), often referred to as "ក្រុមតូច"
in Khmer, is a Cambodian musical group known for creating
original music that blends modern sounds with local cultural
influences. Formed with the goal of bringing a fresh and to a
voice to the Cambodian music scene, the band has a gained
popularity for their unique approach to Cambodian pop music.
Their lyrics often reflect personal stories, social issues, and more
experiences within the country, aiming to resonate audiences.
